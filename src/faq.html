Frequently Asked Questions
SOLIT values your inquieries

Why does it take so long to get to the moon?

Due to regulations by the UNSC (United Nations Space Command) and other signatories, speeds are required to be reduced between the Earth and the Moon. As such, it takes aproximately 3 to 4 hours.

Why have travel times to Mars increased?

Mars is the last stopping point for mining ships heading into the main asteroid belt, as well as a midpoint for mining drones making the long trip to the Kuiper Belt. While Titan is planned to take on most of the drone traffic in the coming decades, for now the space around Mars is rather crowded. As such, plan on 8-12 hours of travel.

Is it safe to visit Mars with the Secunda Domus Project ongoing?

Yes! While certain areas of Mars are off limits, the heavy industry and pollution is intentional. As we continue to clean up our planet from the disasters of the 21st century, the UNC (United Nations Coalition) as well as the few nation-states remaining have agreed to transfer all Co2 producing industries off world to Mars. What is bad for Earth is great for Mars, as over time the excess Co2 will help in our terraforming project. But Unity is well protected with multiple failsafes, as are your complimentary envirosuits.
