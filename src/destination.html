01 Pick your destination

Moon

Enjoy a stunning view of the James Pearson Lunar Elevator as you arrive at the Neil Armstrong Lunar Port. SOLIT's Lunar Package will take you on a historical tour of the moon landings from 1969-1972, as well as ensure you get a beautiful view of earth no matter where your room is. A quick get away for those who don't want to travel too far from home.

Est. Cost for Two

$10,000

Total Time

7 Days

Mars

Earth is our home, but Mars is our first universal triumph, a moment when we declared to the universe that we would no longer limit ourselves to the planet of our origin. Enjoy the legendary nightlife of the Unity Colony or choose to rough it like the earlier colonists. No matter which vacation package you pick, you'll find why people fall in love with our second planet. (Price and duration vary based on package)

Est Cost for Two

$20,000 - $80,000

Total Time

2 weeks - 3 months

Europa

The newly finished SOLIT Europa Resort is now available to you! Enjoy a trip to the surface to stay in one of our state-of-the-art cabins that will protect you from Europa's elements while allowing you to enjoy a beautiful view as you rotate around the King of Planets. For those who don't feel up to staying on the cold surface, the Edward Hubble Europa Station offers some of the finest luxuries in our solar system.

Est Cost for Two

$65,000

Total Time

1 Month

Talk with some of Earth's greatest scientists who descend to the planet to continue the search for life on Titan's surface or who push further to continue to explore our solar system! (At this time, all trips to the surface are for authorized persons only)
