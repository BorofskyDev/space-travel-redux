02Technologies

Shuttle

A Planetary Shuttle (PS) is any vehicle that's capable of entering and leaving the atmosphere of any planet in our solar system. Most shuttles are capable of flight from the Earth to the Moon. SOLIT's PS-275 stands out as a shuttle that can take passengers to Mars and back. Our shuttle will bring you to the Moon, Mars, or to the S-848.

Cruiser

A cruiser is any spacecraft capable of deep space flight. SOLIT's new ST-848 uses the latest technology available to civilian craft to give you the most luxurious trip on your way to Jupiter, Saturn, and one day beyond.

Space Station

A space station is any station that orbits around a planet or moon and is built for habitation. SOLIT has helped finance and build 22 stations. Currently we have 10 around Earth, 2 around the Moon, 8 around Mars, one around Europa and, most recently, one around Titan. We are currently in the works to increase our station presence to 23 stations once the station on Triton (Neptune) is completed.
